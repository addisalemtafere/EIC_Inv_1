Duty Free Exemption

The Duty-Free Exemption permit process is categorized under the following types:

  1. Capital Goods and their Accessories
  2. Construction Materials
  3. Raw Materials
  4. Spare Parts of Capital Goods
  5. Workshop Equipment
  6. Laboratory Equipment
  7. Motor Vehicle

Items 2 and 3 require that a list of pre-approved items need to be uploaded and stored on the central database to serve as a baseline. The Investor prepares the list using an Excel sheet that contains predefined columns. When an Investor requests duty free permit on different instalments,  the system checks that the quantity requested does not exceed the remaining approved balance.

To upload Construction and Raw material baseline items, the following steps are followed.

  1. Select “Upload Construction/Raw Material” from the list of available services on the dashboard. The list of projects belonging to the logged-in Investor are displayed.

 

  1. Press “Continue” and the following Form will be displayed

 

  1. Select “Attach Items List” button to show the “File Open” browser and pick the Excel File that contains the list of items.  The system displays the uploaded items as shown below.

 

  1. Press “Submit Application” to submit the service request. The uploaded items are then approved by authorized Incentive Officer.
