Incentives Management

The Ethiopian Government offers different incentive schemes with a view to encouraging and promoting Investment initiatives in the country.  The incentive schemes depend on different conditions, including:

  • Incentive Type – Customs Duty Exemption or Tax Holiday
  • Investment Sector
  • Region/Address in which the Investment project is located

The above parameters could determine the type and duration of the incentive.

  1. Tax Exemption Incentive request

EIC provides Business Income Tax Exemption Permit for eligible Investment sectors for a specified period as stipulated on the pertinent regulations. The following steps are followed to request online “Tax Exemption” service.

Step 1: Select “Tax Holiday” item from the list of available service menu items. A list of projects registered under the logged-in Investor are listed

Step 2. Select the project for which the “Tax Exemption” is requested

Step 3: Press “Continue” and the list of documents that are required to be attached are listed as shown below.

 

 

Step 4: Attach the necessary documents and click “Submit” button.  

Step 5: Select “Request Form” tab and fill in the required items.
